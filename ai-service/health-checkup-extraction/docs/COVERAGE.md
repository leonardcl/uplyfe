# Biomarker coverage

Every threshold below cites its primary guideline. Lab-to-lab variation exists; thresholds are screening cutoffs, not diagnostic decisions.

## Glucose & HbA1c — ADA 2024

| Biomarker | Normal | Borderline | Abnormal | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fasting glucose (mg/dL) | <100 | 100–125 (IFG) | ≥126 (diabetes range) | ≥400 |
| Random glucose (mg/dL) | — | — | ≥200 | ≥400 |
| 2-hr postprandial / OGTT (mg/dL) | <140 | 140–199 (IGT) | ≥200 | — |
| HbA1c (%) | <5.7 | 5.7–6.4 | ≥6.5 | ≥10.0 |

## Lipids — NCEP ATP III / AHA-ACC 2018

| Biomarker | Desirable | Borderline | High | Very high |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Total cholesterol | <200 | 200–239 | ≥240 | — |
| LDL | <100 | 100–159 | 160–189 | ≥190 |
| HDL — men | ≥40 | — | <40 (low) | — |
| HDL — women | ≥50 | — | <50 (low) | — |
| Triglycerides | <150 | 150–199 | 200–499 | ≥500 (≥1000 = critical) |
| Non-HDL = TC − HDL | <130 | 130–189 | 190–219 | ≥220 |
